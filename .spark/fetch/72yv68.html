Our Approach to Power Trading and Asset Management

At Yggdrasil, our asset management and trading operations are deeply interconnected. We analyze vast amounts of historical weather data and assess how weather forecasts correlate with power prices. This data-driven approach forms the foundation of the algorithms that guide both our trading strategies and asset optimization efforts.

We develop and maintain our own framework for data management and market interaction, ensuring seamless communication with counterparties and efficient handling of physical assets.

Our team trades power in short-term markets across the globe, and this trading expertise directly enhances our ability to manage renewable assets on behalf of our customers. By understanding market dynamics, we make informed decisions that maximize asset performance and value.

Send a request for an offer today for your asset!

Our goal is to trade power using a 100% data-driven and automated approach.

How we work

At Yggdrasil, respect and support are important to us. We respect each other’s expertise and support each other as human beings.

When working with us, you will be part of a team with a true start-up attitude and a flat organisation.

We are committed to our five values. They are the essence of our professional cooperation and how we treat each other.
